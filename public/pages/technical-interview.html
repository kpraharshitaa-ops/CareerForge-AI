🧠 Technical Interview Tips
🔍
Problem Solving Clarify the problem before coding. Think out loud and describe your approach before jumping to the solution.
⏱️
Time Complexity Always mention Big-O complexity for your solution. Compare it to brute force. Aim for optimal but acknowledge trade-offs.
🔲
Edge Cases Consider null inputs, empty arrays, boundary values, and off-by-one errors. Mention them proactively.
🗣️
Communication Explain your reasoning as you answer. Interviewers value clarity of thought as much as correctness.
