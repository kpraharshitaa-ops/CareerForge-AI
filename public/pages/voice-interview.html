🗣️ Voice Interview Tips
📢
Speak Clearly Speak at a natural pace. Avoid mumbling — the microphone picks up what you say, not what you mean.
⭐
Use STAR Structure Situation → Task → Action → Result. This gives your answer a clear arc the AI can evaluate.
⏸️
Use Pause Wisely Pause recording if you need to gather your thoughts. Resume when ready — the transcript accumulates.
⏱️
Aim for 60–120 sec A complete behavioral answer takes 1–2 minutes. Under 30 seconds is usually too short to score well.
